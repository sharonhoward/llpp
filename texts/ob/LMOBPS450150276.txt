To the Right Honourable Barlow Trecothick Lord Mayer of the City of London And the rest of his Majesty's Justices of Oyer and Terminer and General Gaol Delivery for the said City and County of Middlesex in their present Session of Oyer and Terminer and General Gaol Delivery of Justice Hall in the Old Bailey in the Suburbs of the said City Assembled

The Humble Petition of Francis Cuff a Prisoner in his Majesty's Gaol of Newgate for Forgery.

Humbly Sheweth That Your Petitioner now Stands Committed to his Majesty's Gaol of Newgate and Charged in and by the Warrant of The Right Honourable William Beckford late Lord Mayor of the City of London Deceased, by the Oaths of Samuel Harrys and John Weaver and others, for Feloniously Forging a certain Inland Bill of Exchange for the Payment of Fifty Pounds And for Publishing the Same as time in the County of Middlesex with intent to Cheat and Defrand the said John Weaver the supposed Acceptor of the said Bill

Your Petitioner therefore humbly Prays that he may be Bailed, Tried or Discharged at his Present Session, Pursuant to the Statute in Such Case made and provided

And Your Petitioner will ever Pray etc

Thos: Cuff

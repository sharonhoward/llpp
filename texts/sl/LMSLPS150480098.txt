To the Right Honourable St John Thompson Knt Lord Mayor of the City of London and to the Right Worshipfull the rest of his Majestyes Justices of the Peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Sessions Assembled.

The Humble Peticon and Appeale of the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Andrew Undershaft London.

Sheweth That by Virtue of an Order or Pass Warrant under the hands and Seals of two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace for the said City and Libertys thereof (One being of the Quorum) bearing Date the Fourth day of May last Martha the Wife of Solomon Brown and their two Children Eleanor and William were he moved from the Parish of Alhallows Stanning in the City of London aforesaid to the said Parish of Saint Andrew Undershaft London Alledging the said Parish of St Andrew Undershaft to be the Place of their last Legal Settlement

That Your Petitioners Conceiving themselves Aggreived by the said Warrant or Order of the said two Justices Do therefore Appeale to Lordship and Worships therefrom And humbly pray that Your Lordship and Worships will be pleased to hear them in the premisses And that the Church Wardens and Overseers of the Poor of the said Parish of Alhallows Staining London may Appear before you to hear and Abide the Judgment and Determinacon of Your Lordship and Worships touching this Appeale

And Your Petitioners shall ever pray.

S: Butler Agent for the Appellants.

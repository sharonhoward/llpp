To the Worshipfull his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London in their General Quarter Session Assembled.

The Humble Petition & Appeal of the Church Wardens & Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx.

Sheweth That by Vertue of an Order or pass Warrt. under the hands & Seals of the Right Honble. Micajah Perry Lord Mayor of the City of London & Robert Westley Esqr. two of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the said City of London (one whereof being of the Quorum) bearing date the Twelfth day of February last past Mary Powel widow of Walter Powell was removed from the Parish of Saint Botolph Without Aldersgate London unto the Parish of Saint James Clerkenwell in the County of Middx aforesaid as the place of her last Legal Settlement.

That your Petitioners Conceive themselves aggrieved by the said Order or pass & Humbly Appeals against the same.

Your Petitioners therefore Humbly prays Your Worships will please to Appoint a day in this present Sessions That all partys Concerted may Attend to hear & abide the Judgment & determination of this Court touching the said Appeal.

And your Petitioners shall Ever pray Etc

To the Right Honble Sr. Robert Westley Knt. Lord Mayor Sr. Simon Urlin Knt. Recorder and the rest of his Majestys Justices of the peace for the City of London and Libertys thereof in their General Quarter Sessions of the Peace Assembled.

The humble Petition and Appeal of Robert Herne of the parish of Trinity Minories in the Liberty of the Tower of London.

Sheweth That by an Order Dated the 20th: day of March last under the Hands of the said Sr. Robert Westley and Sr. Joseph Hankey Knts. two of his Majesties Justices of the peace for the City of London and Liberties thereof (whereof one is of the Quorum) and both said to reside next unto the Limmitts where the parish Church of the parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate Standeth Your Petitioner is Adjudged the Reputed father of a Male Bastard Child said to be Born on the Body of Sarah Harper Singlewoman on or about the Twenty Sixth day of February last in the said parish of St. Botolph without Aldgate.

And Your Petitioner is therein Ordered to pay to the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor of the said parish the Sume of Four pounds One shilling and Six Pence of lawfull Money of Great Britain for and towards the Maintenance of the said Child And likewise Ordered to pay on Tuesday in every Week from and after the Date thereof so long as the said Child should be Chargeable to the said Parish of Saint Botolph without Aldgate unto the Church Wardens and Overseers of the poor there the sume of Two Shillings and Six pence.

That your Petr. Apprehends himself agrieved by the said Order of the said Two Justices and humbly Appeals to the Court against the same.

Your Petitioner and Appellant therefore prayer that he may be Discharged from the said Order and the same Vacated or that your Petitioner may be relieved in the premisses as this Court Shall see Meet.

And your Petr. Etc

Robt. Herne

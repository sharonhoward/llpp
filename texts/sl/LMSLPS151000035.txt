Sir I Recd a Summons to appear before the Right Honourable the Lord Mayor at Guild Hall of this Day but to my grate misfortune am Afflicted with a Diabetoral complaint a frequency of making Water, with much pain for theere 3 Years past or Is hold be happy to come forward on a publick Business like this as other Men for the govd of my Country. but my being this aflicted hope youll have me paid by

Coleman Street 25 Feby. 1788

I am Sir Your most humble Servt William Barfork

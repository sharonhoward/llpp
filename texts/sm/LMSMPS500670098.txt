To the Rt. Hono. Sr. Francis Child Kt. Lord mayor of Lundun and the Rest of the Homll. Bench now Sitting at Justice hall In the old Bayley

The humll. Pttn. of Edward Carwithrer's now a prisoner In new gate

humbly Sheweth That yr Pttr. was Comitted to newgatt For piracy whenas yr pttr Can prove that he was [...] out of a Ship Called yr James And Thomas of plymouth by one Henery Aurry a pyrott, your pttr.

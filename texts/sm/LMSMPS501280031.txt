To yr Rt Worspp her Matys Justices of ye Peace for ye County of Middx

The humble Peticon of Ruth Hodgkinson of the liberty of Norton Folgate in the said County Widow

Sheweth That yor Petr: for Three Months past was allowed 2s P Weeke As a pension from the said Liberty But the Last Weeke the Same was wholly Taken away from her And for as much as your Petr having Three Small Children to maintained Is not Able to Subsist without Relief from the said Liberty

p Tho: Nash Your Petit: there fore humbly Prays yor Worshpps will be pleased to order That the may be allowed he said Former Pension of 2s P Weeke If yor Worspps soe thinke fitt

And yor Petr: Shall Ever pray Etc

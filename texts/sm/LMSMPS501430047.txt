Sir

you was plesd to ginfe in your order on wensday last to Show cous to their worships why thay haifo not releinted me in my sicknes I sent the order to the Churchwarding of Saint Batrons am he carid is him sew [...] to the churchward is of white chapill but thay would not resein is but [...] it am returnd if to Res [...] a gains and can thay would laick no notis of it wither well thay aford me any relint fonre and my child we must [...] linfe this weaick 8 and 04 ours upon a peney brick that with out slim spedy releife we must both perich from your poor destnesn

Serjant Johonathan Easden

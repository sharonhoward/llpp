Sr Being a very Poor man whose family has Suffer'd very much by this Imprisonment he having no friends in ye. World to Apply him self to whereby to raise money to pay his Fees humbly beggs You'll be so good as to remelt the Court fees & he shall be ever bound to Pray who is wth of Utmt. respect

Clerkenwll Bridewll May ye 1727

Yor most Obedt Servt Thos. Branden

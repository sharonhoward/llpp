I doubt not but you, & the rest of your Brothers, will he ready to assist a Gentleman, as for as ye Law will allow I being in Execution, unprisoned in ye fleer, bey ye my Recognizance may be respited till next Sessions at which term, I will by Haber -us Corpus appears are plead, & try any Indictment yt is, Etc tax be found Agst [...] for I assure you yt I am wrong fully Accused which at a proper [...] I doubt not but as Make appears, your favour & the rest of the Gentlemens in desired by

Sr Yr most Obedt Humble Ser L Saint Lee

Jan ye 15th 1729

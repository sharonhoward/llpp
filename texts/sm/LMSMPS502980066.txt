Whorthy Sir I most humbly beg You'l be Pleasd to Consider [...] My Poor Deptorrable Condittion Almost Starv'd for want having Neither friends nor Allowance, and Also having three Small and helpless Children and bigg with Another, I am Dr Sir Detain'd only for your feese, Mr Willms. has forgiven me his Therefore Most humbly beg for the tenter mercy of god you will be Pleasd According to your Accostom'd goodness &, Charity forgive the Same I and my poor Fartherless Children will as in Duty bound for Ever

Clarkenwell Bridwel Aprill the 13th. 1734

Anne Collins

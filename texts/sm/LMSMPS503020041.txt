Honourd, Sr. I Most humbly beg you'l be so good as to Consider this my Deplorable Condittion being Allmost Starv'd for Want Having no friend in Life to Commisserate this my Un happy and long Confirmt. being About three Months hole and one in New Prison for an Assault and is Now Detain'd onley for Your Honours Fees

I Therefore Most humbly Pray Your honour According to yor't Honours Tends Consideration Goodness and Charity wile be Pleas'd to for give the same and Pyour poor Object wile as in Duty Bound for Ever Pray

Clarton Bridll. July the 21th 1734

Robert Reach

Honoured Sir I make bould to give your Honour an Account of this my Long Confinment hoping your honour will not take the Same himss I was Sent here for an Assault Last Sessions and is now Detain'd only for your honrs. feese

May it therefore Please your honour According to your honrs. Tender Consideration Goodness and Charity Towards poor Prisoners, for give the same as I have no friend in life to Subsist or Mentaine me in this my Miserable Condittion

And I as in Duty bound will for Ever Pray

Charles Thrower

Clartenwll. Bridll. August the 1th. 1734

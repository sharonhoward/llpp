Whorthy Sr. I Most humbly beg you'l be (Please to Consider my Deplorable Condittion being all most Starv'd for want having no Manner of Allowance, and is Detain'd onley for the Court feese and was no friend in Life to Come a near me and as I lye Intirely under your mercy I hope your goodness will be pleas'd to for give the Same It was Indicttd for Stealing a Saustpan Valued one Shillg. and your poor object will for Ever pray

Mary Morgan

Clartenll. Bridll. June the 23th. 1734

Whorthy Sr I Most humbly beg your Honour will not take a miss this my Boldness in taking the Liberty to write to your honour but as Nessecity Oblidges me so to do being these Three Months past Confin'd for an Assault and Allmost Starve Lying on the Beer Boards having no friend in Life to Subsist or Man- tain me my poor helpless Mother being in the Workhouse, therefore beg for the tender Mercy of god as I am Dataind onley for your honour Seese you'l be Pleasd to for give the Same and I as in Duty Bound will for Ever pray

Edward Rowe

Clarten Bridll. July the 24th. 1734

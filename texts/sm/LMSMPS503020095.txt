Whorthy Sr. I Most humbly beg You'l be Pleas'd to Consider my Deplorable Condittion Being All most Starve for Want, and Lying on the Bear boards these three Months past not having any friend in Life to Subsist or Come Near me I Therefore humbly Implore your Goodness will be pleasd, & as am Detaind onley for your lease, to for give the Same and T your poor Object will as in Duty bound for Lod Pray Sr. was Indicted for an Assault and Sent here for three Months to hard Labour

Clarkenwell Bridll. July the 20th. 1734

Thomas Hardgrove

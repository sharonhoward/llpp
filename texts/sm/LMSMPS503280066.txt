To his Majtys. worshipfull Bench of Justices of the Peace for the County of Middx

you are Humbly Desired to Excuse Edward Brightland one of the Headboroughs of white Cross Street (from the service of this Day,) he being soe ill of the Gout that hes [...] not able gett out of his Bed

And yor. Hble Sevt will Ever Pray Edward Bughtland Headboroughs

excused

Jary 14th: 1737

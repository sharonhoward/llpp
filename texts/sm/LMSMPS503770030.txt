Sir I am a poorman that was Fined in Newgate for 3 months which long time being expird for three marks and has laid with a Violent sever a long time haveing no freind in the would to bring me any Belief or Necessaries of Life most Humbley Crave of your Beuortifull Goodness to take [...] court Fees and In hall be in duty son bound to pray Sr. Jovas try'd the 29th of April last at Hicks Hall Sir Jam yr Mr. Obed Humble St.

Joseph Johnson

To Mr. Waller Clk of the Peace at the Temple

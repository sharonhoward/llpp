Worthy Sir

The Ordr. named deo humbly beg leave to acquaint you: that [...] are destrissed to the heith of Extremity hath been confined above Six Months: for An Assault & in a perishing condi [...] on: being al [...] the while upon the bare Boar [...] : and ng Earthly support, but the Goal allowance, are become quite destitute the Kesper: Mr Akerman, seeing the miserable Case of your Objects has forgiven the Goal fess: we humbly pray, that you will of your Usaut Charity: forgive the Court fees; where by, you [...] Objects, might have Liberty we shall beein duty bound to pray for you

Aberham Green

To Mr Wilkinson Cloake of the Peace for Middlelsex

Lincons Inns

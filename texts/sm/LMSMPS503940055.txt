In Pleas [...] My Husband paid ten Shillings and Six pen [...] for my Court fees, and nine Shillings & two pence for my goal fees, and now the Copt. will not let me go untill I pray him twelve Shillings more Wd. I have not therefore I most Humbly pray yr. Honour will of yr. great good nest advice & order it right or as yr. Honr. Seems Meet it will be a great deed of Charity and I will for Ever pray for yr Health & well fare whilst

Hanna Wallpool

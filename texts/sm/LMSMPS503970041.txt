To the Worshipfull his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex in Sessions Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of St. Saviour Southwark in the County of Surry

Humbly Sheweth That Whereas by an Order under the Hands and Seals of Jacob Harvey and William Withers Esqrs. two of his Majesty's Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex Dated 2d Day of August 1749. Elizabeth Cutter Widow was Removed and Convey and from the Parish of Saint Mary Islington in the County of Middlesex to our said Parish of St. Saviour Southwark As the Place of her last Legal Settlement By which We think ourselves Aggrieved

We therefore Do mostly humbly Pray this Court will be Pleased to appoint a time for hearing determining the said Appeal And also to make an Order for all Persons Concerned to Attend the same

And your Petitioners in Duty Bound will ever Pray

Thos Raymond Joshua Greensmith} Churchwardens

Thos. Bowra Thos Dedson} Overseers of the Poor

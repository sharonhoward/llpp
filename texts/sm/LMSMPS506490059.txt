Sr I Should B, Glad if you will be so [...] As to Let me Row Wether this Not is Sorfiehent as Mr. Denton to [...] me it is the Plant of and Defendent An Both in Court and are Waiting for Your Answer I am A poore Woman and if You will Be So Kind as to Get it [...] for me I Shall Ever Pray for You.

St. I am Your Humble Sert. Mary Crocker

Jan. 17th. 1775

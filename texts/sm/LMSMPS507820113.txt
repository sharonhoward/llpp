Worthy Sir

I am an injured Man my life, liberty and Subststance lovelled at by Mr Waddington my prosecutor and next door Neighbour who Keeps or Turners Shop next door to me. This premeditoted Ruin will I hope Appear obvious to you and the Jury that it was there the Villianous Inguilty of him any conflagration happened in my house as I and my Fai [...] were out together spending the Evening. for I Call God to witness I [...] nothing of it - And as my long Imprisonment has rendered Mc incapable of Employing a Councill or Attorney

I humbly Implore you will plead for Me this Day.

And I will Ever pray Etc.

Thomas Patch.

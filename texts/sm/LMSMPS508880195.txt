Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majestys Justices of the peace for the County of Middlesex at their General [---] Session of the Peace Assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Parish of West Ham in the County of Essex

Sheweth That by virtue of an order under the hands and Seals of N Conant and Philip Neve two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing date the twentieth day of March One thousand Seven hundred and ninety three Ann Leechman aged about a year and an half in the said Order Stated to be the lawful Child of George Leechman by Catherine his Wife was on the twenty ninth day of July last removed from the Parish of Ealing in the County of Middlesex unto the Said Parish of West Ham in the County of Essex as the Place of her last legal Settlement

Your Petitions conceiving themselves aggrieved by the Said Order appeal to this Honorable Court against the Same And humbly pray your Worships to appoint a day to hear and determine the Same And your Petitioners Shall ever pray Etc

Hugh Bantocks Thos. Mills Noah Wibrow} Churchwardens

Thos Willis} Overseers

At the General [---] Session of the peace of our Laid the King holden in and for the County of Middlesex on Monday the ninth day of September One thousand Seven hundred and ninety three

It is Ordered that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Par of the Parish of Ealing have Notice of this Appeal and that they and all Parties concerned do attend this Court on Thursday next at nine of the Clock in the forenoon on the matters. of this Petition and Appeal to hear and abide the Judgment and determination of the Said Court touching the Said Appeal

By the Court

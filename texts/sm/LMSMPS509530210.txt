Middlesex To the Worshipful his Majsetys Justices of the Peace for the County of Middlesex at this General Quarter Session of the Peace assembled

The Humble Petition and Appeal of the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish of West Ham in he County of Essex

Sheweth That by virtue of an Order under the hands and Seals of a Mereceron and Daniel Williams Esquires two of his Majestys Justices of the Peace acting in and for the County of Middlesex (one being of the Quorum) bearing Date the third Day of September one thousand Seven hundred and Ninety Eight Joseph Murrell and his two Lawful Children (namely) Edward aged three years or thereabouts nd Frances aged nine Weeks or thereabouts (by his late Wife Elizabeth Deceased) were in the said third Day of September Removed from the Parish of saint Mathew Bethnal green in the County of Middlesex unto the said Parish of west Ham in the County of Essex as the Place of this last legal Settlement

Your Petitioners conceiving themselves agrieved by the said Order appeal to his Honorable Court against the sane and humbly pray your Worships to appoint a Day to hear and Determine the same and your Petitioners shall ever Pray Etc

Richard Rentall William Layal Jno Cutts} Churchwardens

Edwd Francis Joseph Watts} Overseers

At the General Quarter Session of the Peace of our Lord the king holden in and for the County of Middlesex on Monday the Twenty second Day of october one thousand seven hundred and Ninety Eight

It is Ordered that the Churchwardens and Overseers of the Poor of the Parish os Saint Mathew Bethnal Green have Notice of this appeal that they and all Partys Concerned Do attend this Court or Thursday next at Nine of the Clock in the forenoon or the Mathews of this Petition and appeal to hear and abide the Judgment and Determination of the said Court touching the said Appeal.

By the Court
